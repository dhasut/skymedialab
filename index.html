Dictio

Dictio is a cross-platform reading and text-to-speech app. It loads a URL in an embedded browser, extracts readable article text, splits it into sentences and paragraphs, and plays speech from the start or from any sentence you select.

What it does

  • Loads a URL in an embedded browser view
  • Extracts readable article text from the page
  • Structures content into sentences and paragraphs
  • Tap-to-speak from any sentence
  • Multiple TTS engines: native OS TTS, Kokoro ONNX, and Supertonic ONNX
  • Translation from Chinese to English using on device translation
